How to use Care Questions: a short animated guide. A cancer diagnosis can feel overwhelming, but you do not have to figure it all out at once. Your journey is broken into five steps: after being diagnosed, choosing treatment, during treatment, after treatment, and integrative therapy. In each step you get two things: a checklist of questions to print and bring to your doctors, and safe, helpful websites. When you are ready, start here with a short list of questions.
